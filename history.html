History

Since 1885, the Dr. Pepper logo has changed more than ten times.

Founded by pharmacist Charels Alderon in Waco Texas, the drink took popularity within years. From the mixing of random flavors in 1885 to the establishment in 1891, its first introduction to the pulbic in 1904, and the company's lawsuit with Coca-Cola to now, Dr. Pepper has become a world-wide franchise.


10-2-4 Slogan: During WWII, a scientific study suggested that energy levels dip around 10 a.m., 2 p.m., and 4 p.m., leading to the "10-2-4" slogan encouraging Dr Pepper consumption.

Secret Formula: Dr Pepper's formula is kept in two separate bank vaults, with no one person knowing the entire recipe.
