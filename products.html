Products

In additon to the classic Dr. Pepper, Diet, and Zero Sugar variations, the company has also come out with limited edition flavors over the years.

The most recent new flavor is Dr. Pepper Blackberry, in its regular, diet, and zero forms. Brought back for the summer is Dr. Pepper Creamy Coconut, a play on the dirty soda trend, where people add creamer and syrups to their sodas. Companies like Swig and Fiiz are large players in the Utah market, profiting off of their fun soda combinations.
